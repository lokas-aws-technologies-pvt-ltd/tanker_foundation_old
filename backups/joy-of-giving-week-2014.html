Joy of Giving Week - 2014

Participated in the Battle of the Buffet Event (BOB) on 1st October 2014 at Chennai Trade Centre. TANKER raised Rs. 38 Lakhs through this event. This amount will be utilized for underprivileged people with kidney ailments.

We put up WISH TREES at ABAN Groups, Savera Hotel and Ascendas, Chennai
between 1st October to 7th October 2014. Activities as follows:

  • Brochures and leaflets distributed to all visitors.
  • TANKER awareness posters and charts were displayed in our stall.
