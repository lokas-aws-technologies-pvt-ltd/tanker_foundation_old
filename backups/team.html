Board of Trustees

Dr. Georgi Abraham
Founder-Trustee, TANKER Foundation

MD, FRCP (UK) Prof. of Medicine, PIMS Pondicherry, Consultant Nephrologist & Transplant Physician, MMM Hospital, Chennai

Dr. Georgi Abraham is a dedicated, service-oriented, humble and renowned Nephrologist, who has done exceptional and distinguished service in the field of Nephrology. Seeing the effects of kidney disease on his patients and the difference awareness and early detection can make, Dr. Abraham launched TANKER Foundation. With help from other Members of the Board and drawing from his own in-depth knowledge, Dr. Abraham hopes to help underprivileged kidney patients and also take concrete steps to lower the incidence of the disease among the general public.

Mr. Suresh Balaje
Chairman

Film Producer & Director, Pranaamam Pictures, Chennai.

Suresh Balaje is an Indian film producer, primarily concentrating on Malayalam and Tamil films. He is the son of yesteryear producer and actor K. Balaji. Suresh Balaje’s father had two kidney transplants. So, he knows what it is to have a kidney patient at home. Apart from that he has a passion for serving kidney patients which is why he leads TANKER Foundation from the front.

Mrs. Latha A. Kumaraswami
Managing Trustee

Director Quadra Press

Latha was instrumental in setting up the Tamilnad Kidney Research (TANKER) Foundation in 1993, along with Dr Georgi Abraham who was treating her grand mother-in-law, freedom fighter and founder of children’s home, Bala Mandir, in Chennai, Manjubhashini. Latha’s background in PR helps in running the Foundation while her passion stems from seeing the trauma of kidney disease up close.

Mrs. Revathy K. Asha
Trustee

Film Director, Actor & Social Activist

Revathy is an Indian film actress and director, known for her works predominantly in Tamil and Malayalam cinema. She has won several accolades, including National Film Award in three different categories, and has bagged six Filmfare awards in the south. Revathy is also known to be a socially-responsible person willing to serve the needy in her own capacity and has experience with a wide variety of philanthropic organisations. This makes her a very valuable member of the Board.

Mrs. Latha Pandiarajan
Trustee

Ma Foi Strategic Consultants

Latha Pandiarajan, the co-founder of recruitment firm Ma Foi, is giving back to society through her own foundation, MaFoi Trust, which runs an educational programme and a sports academy for children, provides training and employment opportunities to the unemployed, and also trains women to become entrepreneurs. It is a natural corollary for her to be involved with TANKER Foundation.

Mr. Reji Abraham
Trustee

Director, ABAN Group of Companies

Reji Abraham is the Managing Director of Aban Group in India. He found place in the Forbes list of the world's wealthiest people in the years 2008 and 2009. He also runs India's largest offshore drilling company, Aban Offshore. His place in TANKER Foundation is not just based on the fact that a family member is a kidney patient but also that he feels the urge to help kidney patients in need.

Dr. Sumantran
Trustee

Chairman, Celeris Technologies

Dr Sumantran is that unique combination of a business leader, technocrat, an academician and author, having lived and worked in the US, Europe and Asia over a 30-plus years career. He is Chairman of Celeris Technologies and an advisor to several Fortune-100 organizations in auto, technology, industrial equipment and aerospace sectors. He has also been designated as Independent Director of Indigo India. But why he is a trustee of TANKER Foundation is his empathy for the work the Foundation does as he is a kidney donor himself.

Mrs. Nina Reddy
Trustee

Director, Savera Hotels

Nina Reddy dons several hats. She is a hotelier, a socialite, a connoisseur of decor, a businesswoman, a philanthropist and an inspiration to women.
